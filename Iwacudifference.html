Our Story

It is an idea of Steven MUNYEJABO for helping Rwandans after spending remarkable time in nursing care in Canada, New Brunswick.

He thought of something that could help old people living with frailty within the Rwandan community, either abroad or in Rwanda.

He came up with this big idea of providing the best home care services to old mothers and fathers wishing to return to their homeland and those already in Rwanda but needing a more comfortable home with professional and well-trained home carers.

Cultural Values
